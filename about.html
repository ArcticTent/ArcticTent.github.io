Who we are:

Welcome to Arctic Tent Studios, where passion meets creativity and innovation!
We are a very small, but dedicated team, consisting of 3 indie game developers with a shared vision of crafting immersive and unforgettable gaming experiences.
Whether it's fun, melancholy or unexplored realms you are looking for, we strive to give you the best entertainment possible and inspire players worldwide with new and interesting game ideas.

Our Story:

The Story of Arctic Tent Studios began in 2019 when a few students came together and decided to combine their different ideas and... well ... make games!

It started out as some small school projects and prototypes, but over time became a bigger and bigger passion.

So much so that in 2021, we decided to take the first step and officially start our production on a full game release of a prototype that had been on our minds for a while.

Since then, we've embarked on an exciting adventure, overcoming challenges, celebrating successes, and learning and growing with the project and coming ever closer to releasing it worldwide.

Our Goal:

At Arctic Tent, our mission is simple: to craft exceptional games that captivate, inspire, and delight. While everyone of our team is still a student working in their spare time, making the process of releasing games a bit slower, we believe we can still convince with good storytelling and will try to immerse our players with attention to detail at all the right places.
