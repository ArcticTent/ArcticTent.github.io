Contact Us

We love hearing from our players, fellow developers, and anyone else interested in what we do! Whether you have questions, feedback, or just want to say hello, we're here and ready to chat. Below are the various ways you can get in touch with us:

Community Discord Server:

If you not only want to connect with us, but also get to know fellow gamers and game enthusiasts, be sure to check out our own Community Discord Server. Here, we have several dedicated channels to share the newest updates for our games, announce Events and interact with our community by exchanging ideas and giving you the chance to connect with likeminded people. You're always welcome!
